# How Does Computer Work ?

  • As we know our computer work on this language of 0 and 1 called binary which really acts as a transtior which then can later form Logic gates and then modules and finnaly a chip which then will become the head of our computer.

  • Transtior: It is a Normal object which determines weather the object is on an on state or 1 and off state or 0.

  • Logic Gates: When the transitor determines a number, then we can take the two values and from pattern resulting in logic gates which can perform various process. some of the famous logic gates our NAND, AND, NOT a etc.

  • Modules: Now using the logic gates and arranging them in specific patterns we can then make Module which can then perfom acts such as Arthemetic and etc.

# Units of Bits & Bytes

Number of Bits(b) & Bytes(B) Name or Unit
A Binary Digit (1 or 0 ) 1 bit
4 Bits 1 nibble
8 Bits or 2 nibble 1 byte
1024 bytes (2^10) 1 Kilobyte or 1 KB
1024 Kbytes (2^20) 1 Megabyte or 1 MB
1024 Mbytes (2^30) 1 Gigabyte or 1 GB
1024 Gbytes (2^40) 1 Terabyte or 1 TB
1024 Tbytes (2^50 ) 1 Petabyte or 1 PB
1024 Pbytes (2^60 ) 1 Exabyte or 1 EB
1024 Ebytes (2^70) 1 Zetabyte or 1 ZB
1024 Zbytes (2^80) 1 Yottabyte or 1 YB
